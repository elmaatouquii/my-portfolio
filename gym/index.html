Beginner Workout Plan

Day 1: Full Body

  • Squats: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Push-ups: 3 sets of 8 reps
  • Bent-over rows: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Plank: 3 sets of 20 seconds

Day 2: Upper Body

  • Push-ups: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Dumbbell shoulder press: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Bicep curls: 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Tricep dips: 3 sets of 10 reps

Day 3: Lower Body

  • Squats: 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Lunges: 3 sets of 10 reps per leg
  • Calf raises: 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Glute bridges: 3 sets of 12 reps

Nutrition Tips

Protein Intake

Aim for 1.6-2.2 grams of protein per kg of body weight. Include sources like chicken, fish, eggs, and legumes.

Hydration

Drink at least 8 glasses of water daily. Proper hydration supports metabolism and recovery.

Healthy Habits

Eat balanced meals with vegetables, fruits, whole grains, and healthy fats. Avoid processed foods and sugary drinks.
